What is Prickly Palz?

Prickly Palz is a collection of 5,555 of the cutest cacti you’ve ever seen!

Owning a Prickly Pal will give you a guaranteed whitelist for all quarterly SEASONAL HARVESTS.

A surprise MID-SEASON CROP will also release every quarter, which will be available at low cost to all owners of that season’s harvest!

We will plant 100,000 trees after every Seasonal Harvest- that’s 400,000 trees a year!
